BASIC RULES

You should believe in yourself, train and achieve your goal of fitness. Here are some rules one should follow.

  1. Eat at least five times a day, every two to three hours.
  2. Have protein drink immediately after workout.
  3. Drink atleast 3 litres of water a day.
  4. Do not rely on supplements to do the work for you EAT.
  5. Do not eat large amounts of food before you sleep.
