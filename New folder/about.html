ABOUT

Fitness Zone is a worldwide fitness website founded by Varun Verma, Deepak Jolly and Sanchit Malik. The main motive of developing this website is to encourage people towards Fitness.So,they decide to develop a website through which people can do exercises anywhere they like .Fitness Zone also provides meal plans to viewers according to there needs.Everyhing is absolutely free.

History

Fitness Zone was founded by Varun Verma, an alumnus of Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad Prior to setting up Fitness Zone, Sanchit had worked with GE Capital as vice president of business development (retail), as well as serving stints with ABN AMRO Bank and AMF Bowlinghai. Fitness Zone was launched in the US marke in 2014 to cater to the overseas Indian community for their Fitness needs. The founding team consisted of Deepak Jolly, Mohanjeet (Co-Founder & Chief Commercial Officer), Sarabjeet Co-Founder & CEO - India, formerly Chief Operating Officer & Chief Financial Officer) and Sachin Bhatia (ex-Chief Marketing Officer). After serving 2 years, since the company's inception, Sachin Bhatia quit Fitness Zone as an active member and decided to work as an independent advisor and a prime shareholder in the company.

In 2016, the company strengthened focus on the mobile route by creating several fitness-related Apps for all types of mobile devices (smartphones and basic cellphones). In June 2017 Fitness Zone invested $3 Million Dollars in Inspirock, a diet and workout planning website to improve workout planner.Sarabjeet was appointed as CEO - India in August 2017.

Our Goal

Our goal is to build fit and healthy society and to reach out to common masses who cannot afford world class gym facilities.
