Full Body Workout

Abdominal Crunches (x8) Watch Video >>

Lie on your back with your knees bent and your arms stretched forward.Then lift your upper body off the floor. Hold for a few seconds and slowly return.It primarily works the rectus abdomininis muscle and the obliques.


Sit-Ups (x14) Watch Video >>

Lie on your back with your hands behind your ears. Then lift your upper body off the floor and slowly up to the sitting position. Don't tug your neck when you get up. Slowly go back to the start position and repeat the exercise. If your spine hurts,please change to another homeworkout.


Leg Raises (x6) Watch Video >>

Lie down on your back,and put your hands beneath your hips for support. Then lift your legs up until they form a right angle with the floor. Slowly bring your legs back down and repeat the exercise.


Plank ( 30s) Watch Video >>

Lie on the floor with your toes and forearms on the ground. Keep your body straight and hold this position as long as you can. This exercise strengthens the abdomen, back and shoulders.

Reverse Crunches (x30) Watch Video >>

Lie on your back with your knees up at a 90 degree angle and your hands behind your head. Lift your upper body and thighs, and then stretch out.Repeat this exercise.


Cross Arm Crunches (x30) Watch Video >>

Lie down and bend your knees with your feet flat on the floor. Cross your arms in front of your chest. Then lift your head and shoulders up to make a 30 degree angle with the ground. It primarily works on the rectus abdominis muscle and obliques.

Arms Workout

Arm Circles (x15) Watch Video >>

Stand on the floor with your arms extended straight out to the sides at shoulder height. Move your arms forward in circles, and then move backwards.


Push-Ups (x5) Watch Video >>

Lay prone on the ground with arms supporting your body. Keep your body straight while raising and lowering your body with your arms. This exercise works the chest, shoulders, triceps, back and legs.


Triceps Dips (x10) Watch Video >>

For the start position,sit on the chair. Then move your hip off the chair with your hands holding the edge of the chair. Slowly bend and stretch your arms to make your body go up and down. This is a great exercise for triceps.


Mountain Climber (x10) Watch Video >>

Start in the push-up position. Bend your right knee towards your chest and keep your left leg straight, then quickly switch from one leg to the other. This exercise strengthens multiple muscle groups.

Leg Workout

Curtsy Lunges (x20) Watch Video >>

Stand straight up. Then step back with your left leg to the right, and bend your knees at the same time. Go back to the start position and switch to the other side.


Bottom Leg Lift Left (x15) Watch Video >>

Lie on your left side with your head resting on your left hand. Then put your right foot forward on the floor. Lift your left leg up and down.


Bottom Leg Lift Right (x15) Watch Video >>

Lie on your right side with head resting on your right hand. Then put your left foot forward on the floor. Lift your right leg up and down.


Bulgarian Slpit Squat Left (x15) Watch Video >>

Stand several feet away from a bench. Rest your left foot on the bench behind you. Lower your body until your back knee nearly touches the floor and your front thigh is parallel to the floor. Then go back to the start position and repeat the exercise. There should be enough space between your front foot and the bench so that your front knee does not go over your toes. keep your upper body straight. It's a great exercise for the gluteus and hamstrings.


Bulgarian Slpit Squat Right (x15) Watch Video >>

Stand several feet away from a bench. Rest your right foot on the bench behind you. Lower your body until your back knee nearly touches the floor and your front thigh is parallel to the floor. Then go back to the start position and repeat the exercise. There should be enough space between your front foot and the bench so that your front knee does not go over your toes. keep your upper body straight.


Wall Sit (x20) Watch Video >>

Start with your back against the wall, then slide down until your knees are at a 90 degree angle. Keep your back against the wall with your hands and arms away from your legs. Hold the position. The exercise is to strengthen the quadriceps muscles.
